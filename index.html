Our Story

Your Neighbourhood Destination for Premium Chai

Welcome to Chaayekhana DHA 1 — an official franchise of Pakistan's beloved Chaayekhana, nestled on Defence Avenue, DHA Phase 1, Islamabad.

Whether you're catching up with friends, enjoying family time, grabbing a quick breakfast, or looking for a cozy spot to work remotely — our café offers a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like a home away from home.

We carry the authentic legacy of Chaayekhana into your neighbourhood, serving the same legendary chai and premium menu you love, just around the corner.
